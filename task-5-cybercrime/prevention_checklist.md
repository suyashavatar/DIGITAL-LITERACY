## Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
##✅ Do’s
1. Use strong and unique passwords

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

3.Turn on 2FA for email, banking, and social media accounts for an extra layer of security.

4. Verify links before clicking.

5.Always check the URL of websites and avoid clicking on suspicious or shortened links.

6.Keep your devices updated.

7.Regularly update your phone and laptop to protect against security vulnerabilities.

8.Avoid accessing sensitive accounts (like banking apps) on public Wi-Fi.

9. Be cautious while sharing personal information. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority.

10. Regularly monitor your bank transactions.

11. Check your account statements frequently to detect any unauthorized activity early.

12.Install apps only from trusted sources

13.Use official app stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.


##❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t respond to unknown calls/messages about money or prizes.These are often scams designed to trick you into sharing sensitive information.

2.Don’t save passwords or card details on shared devices.

3.Always log out after using public or shared computers.
